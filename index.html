Welcome To Arun Exim

Arun Exim is a trusted coconut-based products exporter rooted in the agricultural heritage of Chinnampalayam, Namakkal District, Tamil Nadu, India. Although our export journey officially began in 2026, our connection with coconut cultivation spans more than 50 years of farming excellence. For generations, our family has been dedicated to cultivating high-quality coconuts across 50+ acres of fertile farmland, combining traditional farming practices with modern agricultural techniques. This long-standing experience has given us deep expertise in coconut cultivation, harvesting, quality control, and product handling. At Arun Exim, we take pride in offering premium coconut products sourced directly from our farms and a strong network of trusted local vendors. Our extensive vendor partnerships enable us to meet diverse customer requirements while maintaining consistent quality and reliable supply. We are committed to delivering fresh, natural, and sustainably sourced coconut products to international markets. Our focus on quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a dependable partner for businesses seeking authentic coconut products from India. With decades of farming knowledge, a robust cultivation base, and a growing export network, Arun Exim aims to bring the richness of Indian coconuts to customers around the world. We believe in building long-term relationships through trust, transparency, and excellence in every shipment we deliver. From the coconut farms of Chinnampalayam to global markets — Arun Exim is committed to sharing nature's finest coconut products with the world.
